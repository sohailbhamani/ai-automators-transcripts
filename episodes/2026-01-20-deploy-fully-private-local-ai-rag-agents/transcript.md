# DEPLOY Fully Private + Local AI RAG Agents

When you upload documents to an AI service, you're placing a lot of trust in that company. Trust that they'll keep those documents secure and that they won't end up exposed in a data breach down the road. For companies handling sensitive information like financial, legal, medical, or client docs, trust me, just isn't good enough. And that's why more and more companies are looking to deploy fully local airgapped AI systems. With this type of system, you have complete data sovereignty with zero external API calls. And while there is an upfront cost to build out the infrastructure, your sensitive documents stay exactly where they belong on your servers.
